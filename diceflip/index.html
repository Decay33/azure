How to Play

  1. Use arrow keys to flip adjacent dice and align three or more matching faces.
  2. Each move consumes a directional arrow — earn more every three levels.
  3. Build combos to charge powerups like wild dice and 3x3 bombs.
  4. Complete objectives before running out of moves to progress to the next level.
